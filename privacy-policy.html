Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 6, 2026

1. About the App

OfferteSenzaSforzi displays product offers and links to Amazon. No account or login is required. An internet connection is necessary to retrieve current offers.

2. Data We Process

We do not ask for your name, password, payment details, contacts, photos, or location. When you use the app, our technical providers may process basic network information, such as your IP address, device or app version, request time, and error logs.

If you enable category notifications, we process your selected categories and a pseudonymous push-notification token. App preferences may also be stored locally on your device.

3. How We Use and Share Data

We use this information only to display offers, send notifications you request, maintain security, and resolve technical problems. The legal bases are your consent for optional notifications and our legitimate interest in operating the app.

Data may be processed by hosting and push-notification providers, including Google Firebase Cloud Messaging or Apple Push Notification service where applicable. We do not sell personal data or use it for targeted advertising.

4. Amazon Affiliate Links

As an Amazon Associate, OfferteSenzaSforzi earns from qualifying purchases. The app uses affiliate links, so we may receive a commission when you make a qualifying purchase, at no additional cost to you.

When you open an Amazon link, Amazon processes data under its own privacy policy. We do not receive your Amazon login, payment, shipping, or individual order details. Affiliate links contain our referral identifier, and Amazon may provide us with aggregate reports that do not identify the purchaser.

5. Notifications and Retention

Notifications are optional and can be disabled in the app or device settings. Notification tokens and category choices are kept only while needed to provide alerts. Technical logs and support messages are retained only as long as needed for security, support, or legal obligations.

6. Your Rights and Contact

Under applicable data-protection law, you may request access, correction, deletion, restriction, or portability of your data, object to processing, or withdraw consent. You may also complain to your local data-protection authority.
