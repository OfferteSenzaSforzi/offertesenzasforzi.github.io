Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 13, 2026

1. About the App

OfferteSenzaSforzi displays product offers and links to Amazon. No account or login is required. An internet connection is necessary to retrieve current offers.

2. Data We Process

We do not ask for your name, password, payment details, contacts, photos, or location. When you use the app, our server and technical providers necessarily process basic network information, such as your IP address, request time, requested resource, and technical error logs.

The app stores tutorial completion and favorite product identifiers locally on your device. These preferences are not uploaded to our server.

3. How We Use and Share Data

We use network information only to display offers, deliver product images, maintain security, and resolve technical problems. The legal bases are providing the service you request and our legitimate interest in operating and protecting it.

Data may be processed by our hosting providers and by Amazon when its product images or links are requested. The app does not include advertising, analytics, tracking, Firebase, or push-notification services. We do not sell personal data or use it for targeted advertising.

4. Amazon Affiliate Links

As an Amazon Associate, OfferteSenzaSforzi earns from qualifying purchases. The app uses affiliate links, so we may receive a commission when you make a qualifying purchase, at no additional cost to you.

When you open an Amazon link, Amazon processes data under its own privacy policy. We do not receive your Amazon login, payment, shipping, or individual order details. Affiliate links contain our referral identifier, and Amazon may provide us with aggregate reports that do not identify the purchaser.

5. Local Storage and Retention

Tutorial and favorite preferences remain on your device until you remove them or uninstall the app. Technical logs and support messages are retained only as long as needed for security, support, or legal obligations. The app does not create an account, so there is no remote account to delete.

6. Your Rights and Contact

Under applicable data-protection law, you may request access, correction, deletion, restriction, or portability of your data, object to processing, or withdraw consent. You may also complain to your local data-protection authority.
